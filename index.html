Crystal Collector!

How to play Crystal Collector

  1. You will be given a random target number at the beginning of the game.
  2. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  3. You win the game by matching the target number. You lose if your total score goes above the target number.
  4. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  5. Each time the game starts, the game will choose a new random number and the crystals will have new values.
